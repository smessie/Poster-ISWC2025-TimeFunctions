Time Functions enable consistent
and type-agnostic comparisons

https://w3id.org/time-fn#

With "2025-08"^^xsd:gYearMonth AS ?tL

  • tfn:periodMinInclusive(?tL)   → "2025-08-01T00:00:00.000+14:00"^^xsd:dateTime
  • tfn:periodMinExclusive(?tL)  → "2025-07-31T23:59:59.999+14:00"^^xsd:dateTime
  • tfn:periodMaxInclusive(?tL)  → "2025-08-31T23:59:59.999-14:00"^^xsd:dateTime
  • tfn:periodMaxExclusive(?tL) → "2025-09-01T00:00:00.000-14:00"^^xsd:dateTime
  • tfn:bindDefaultTimezone(?tL, "+02:00") → "2025-08+02:00"^^xsd:gYearMonth
